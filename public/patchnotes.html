Patch Notes

🐉 v2.0.1 · March 7, 2026
Overview
◆v2.0.1 is a major balance and content update for Dragon's Horizon. This patch overhauls mob spawn density across five mod configs, tunes Dreadsteel into a true endgame armor set, expands the modlist with boss and identity mods, removes an unbalanceable mod, and improves quality-of-life with client-side additions. Waystones are now permanently unbreakable.
✅ Added Mods
Client Only
Install on client only. Server does not require these.
◆Better Fog — Enhanced atmospheric fog for a more immersive world
◆Legendary Tooltips — Rarity-styled tooltip borders and item presentation
◆No Chat Reports — Strips cryptographic chat signatures — prevents player reporting
◆My Server Is Compatible — Bypasses server compatibility checks for extra client-side mods
Both (Server + Client)
Must be installed on both the server and all clients.
◆Advancement Plaques — Stylish animated pop-up cards when players earn advancements
◆Bosses' Rise — New powerful boss encounters scattered across the world
◆Origins — Choose a race/origin with unique passive traits at world start
◆Strictly Origins — Restricts items and actions based on your chosen Origin
🗑️ Removed Mods
◆Vikings — No config files available. Unable to tune spawn behaviour or balance stats alongside the other mods in this pack. Removed to maintain config consistency across the modlist.
⚖️ Nerfed / Buffed (Config Changes)
Mob Spawn Weights — 50% Reduction (Nerf)
All mob spawn weights across 5 config files have been halved to reduce world overcrowding and improve server TPS when multiple mob mods are active simultaneously.
↓alexsmobs — Straddler: 70 → 35 · Rocky Roller: 60 → 30 · Underminer: 50 → 25 · Gazelle/Mimicube: 40 → 20 · Guster: 35 → 17
↓alexsmobs — Elephant/Blobfish/Soul Vulture/Tiger: 30 → 15 · Gorilla/Kangaroo: 25 → 12 · All others proportional (~50%)
↓alexscaves — Cave Creature Modifier: 1.75 → 0.875
↓goblintraders — Overworld Goblin: 25% → 12% · Vein Goblin: 25% → 12%
↓mobs_of_mythology — Kobold: 70 → 35 · Kobold Warrior: 60 → 30 · Chupacabra: 30 → 15 · Drake: 10 → 5 · Sporeling: 16 → 8
↓graveyard — Revenant/Ghoul/Skeleton Creeper: 25 → 12 · Reaper: 5 → 2 · Nightmare: 10 → 5 · Horde Spawn Attempts: 40 → 20
Dreadsteel Armor — Endgame Tier (Buff)
Dreadsteel has been reworked from a mid-tier set into a true post-Ender Dragon endgame armor. Stats now exceed Netherite, making it the reward for the most demanding late-game content.
↑Helmet Armor: 3 → 12 · Chestplate: 7 → 18 · Leggings: 8 → 15 · Boots: 3 → 11
↑Armor Toughness: 3 → 10 · Knockback Resistance: 0.10 → 0.35
↑Scythe Attack Damage: 12 (13 tooltip) → 38 (39 tooltip) · Attack Speed: 1.0 → 1.4
Waystones — Unbreakable (Balance)
Waystones are now set to Unbreakable in the config. Players' fast-travel networks can no longer be destroyed by accidents or griefing.
📊 Balance Notes
◆Mob Density — With 5+ mob mods active, halving spawn weights brings total mob pressure closer to a vanilla-equivalent feel while keeping the world diverse.
◆Dreadsteel vs Netherite — Full Dreadsteel (56 total armor) now sits well above Netherite (20 total armor), reflecting its post-endgame status. Toughness of 10 makes it especially effective vs boss attacks.
◆Origins Integration — Origins adds passive traits at world start with no stat inflation. Strictly Origins enforces role boundaries. Together they add identity without breaking combat balance.
◆Bosses' Rise — New boss encounters provide a power curve target for the Dreadsteel grind — players are expected to gear up before engaging them.
◆Goblin Traders — Spawn chance reduced from 25% to 12% per attempt. Goblin traders remain available but are now a noteworthy encounter rather than a constant presence.
◆Graveyard Hordes — Horde spawn attempts halved (40→20). Graveyard biomes remain dangerous but no longer spawn overwhelming numbers simultaneously.
🔧 Bug Fixes
✓Waystones — Waystone blocks could be broken by players or explosions, destroying fast-travel networks. Now set to Unbreakable.
✓Chat Reports — Player chat messages carried cryptographic signatures enabling Mojang report system. No Chat Reports mod removes signatures server-wide.
✓Client Mod Compatibility — Clients with extra client-side mods were rejected by the server. My Server Is Compatible resolves the version mismatch check.
✓Server TPS — Excess mob spawn pressure from stacked mob mods caused TPS drops in loaded chunks. Spawn weight reduction across all 5 configs addresses this.
⚠️ Known Issues
⚖Creeper Overhaul — Spawn weight config uses boolean toggles only — numeric spawn rate tuning is not available without datapack edits. Creeper density unchanged.
⚖Illager Invasion — No numeric spawn weights exist in config. Illager raid composition toggles only. Raid frequency is controlled by vanilla Bad Omen mechanics.
⚖Savage & Ravage — Config only contains behaviour toggles (evoker totems, vex health, AI). Raid spawn frequency not adjustable via config in this version.
