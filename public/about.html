About the Modpack

Dragon Horizons

Dragon Horizons is a Forge 1.20.1 modpack built around a single question — what if Minecraft survival felt like a Soulslike? Mobs scale as you explore. Your gear is locked behind real investment. Bosses punish overconfidence. Legendary weapons don't drop from crafting tables. They drop from the things that hunt you.

Core Pillars

Design Philosophy

⚔️
Soulslike Combat
Marius Soulslike brings posture mechanics, bleed buildup, weapon arts, and 9 original bosses. ArmorcCurve caps armor at 75% — every hit lands.
📈
Living Difficulty
AutoLeveling scales mob stats by distance from spawn. A creeper near your base is manageable. The same creeper 3000 blocks out has earned respect.
🧠
Earned Progression
Reskillable gates all gear tiers behind 8 skill disciplines. The Player Skill Tree converts kills into permanent power. Nothing is given.
🐉
A World That Lives
Ice and Fire, Alex's Mobs, Alex's Caves, and Mobs of Mythology fill every biome with things that don't wait to be found.
🗡️
Weapons With Weight
Spartan Weaponry and Marius Soulslike span iron daggers to legendary greatswords. Each tier locked behind real investment.
🏆
Built for Multiplayer
All configs tuned for a shared server. Mob caps, invasion rates, and attribute ceilings are punishing but fair for a group.
